http://ffmpeg.zeranoe.com/builds/win32/shared/ffmpeg-20141117-git-3f07dd6-win32-shared.7z

http://ffmpeg.zeranoe.com/builds/win64/shared/ffmpeg-20141117-git-3f07dd6-win64-shared.7z
